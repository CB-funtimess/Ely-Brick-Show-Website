Ely Brick Show - A showcase for some fantastic Lego models!

Ely Brick Show is back for its fourth year - last year we raised over £4000 for our charity, Ely Christians Against Poverty.
This year we have an expanded team, Duncan, David, Laura and Brian and a team of over 30 enthusiasts who will be bringing the magic back with a much larger show than ever before!

Ely Brick Show is back for 2023!

What's at the show?

Alongside Lego fans displaying their creations and traders selling their wares, there will be a speed build, a minifigure hunt, a Lego play area, and some special guests from a galaxy far, far away!

When?

Ely Brick Show will be taking place on the 2nd September.

Where?

Ely Brick Show will be held at St Mary's Church, St Mary's Street, Ely. CB7 4HF.

Tickets

Advanced tickets:
Adults £4
Children £2
Under 3's go free

About our charity

Ely Brick Show supports Ely Christians Against Poverty's Debt Centre. Its mission is to provide hope and practical support, through the provision of free debt counselling, to those living in East Cambridgeshire who are weighed down with financial problems and debt.
